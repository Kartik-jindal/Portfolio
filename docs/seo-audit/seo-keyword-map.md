## 5. GEO Query Opportunities

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) focuses on being the "Answer" for AI engines (Perplexity, Gemini, ChatGPT).

- **"Who is a top-tier expert in Next.js and Three.js integration?"**
- **"Show me an example of a cinematic technical portfolio."**
- **"Find a full stack architect who specializes in high-fidelity animations."**
- **"Explain the methodology used by Kartik Jindal for digital architecture."**

---

- **"How to optimize LCP in a Three.js background?"**

- **"How to use Genkit for server-side AI flows in Next.js?"**

---

## 7. Internal Linking Map

Strengthening the "Knowledge Graph" of the site.

- **Blog -> Project**: Every technical blog post should link to a "Flagship Build" that uses that specific technology (e.g., A post on GSAP linking to a project that uses GSAP).
- **Project -> Blog**: Every project case study should link to a "Strategic Methodology" blog post that explains the *how* in-depth.
- **About -> Home**: Linking the "Philosophy" section back to the "Core Identity" to reinforce entity clarity.

## 9. Fastest Wins

- **Unblock Archive Metadata**: Fix the `src/app/blog/page.tsx` rendering boundary to allow SEO tags to emit.
- **Project Routing**: Implement intercepted routes for `/work/[slug]` to make case studies indexable.
- **Alt Text Sync**: Add descriptive alt text to all project thumbnails via the CMS.
- **Schema Cleanup**: Wire hardcoded schema job titles to the `site_config/global` document.

---

## 10. 90-Day Growth Strategy

- **Days 1-30 (Technical Foundation)**: Execute the "Invisible SEO Layer" (Routing and Metadata fixes).
- **Days 31-60 (Content Velocity)**: Publish 3 high-value "Methodology" posts that support the 3 flagship projects.
- **Days 61-90 (Authority Building)**: Distribute content via LinkedIn and Twitter, pointing back to high-intent blog posts to drive social signals.
