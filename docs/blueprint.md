# **App Name**: Kartik Jindal | Cinematic Portfolio

## Core Features:

- Cinematic Hero & Global Interactions: Develop a highly interactive and visually striking hero section featuring a 3D object/scene, custom cursor, magnetic buttons, and ambient motion, serving as the immersive entry point.
- Narrative Projects Showcase: Implement a dynamic projects section utilizing scroll-driven choreography, pinned sections, and layered transitions to tell compelling case studies for flagship work, with a lighter section for smaller projects.
- Refined Personal Brand Sections: Craft clean, editorial 'About,' 'Skills,' and 'Experience' sections with strong typographic hierarchy, subtle animations, and curated presentation to build credibility and showcase expertise.
- Elegant Testimonials Display: Create a premium and trustworthy display for testimonials, presented as compact cards or an elegant carousel with strong typography.
- Polished Contact & Conversion Points: Design a strong concluding contact section with clear calls-to-action (CTAs) for email and social links, along with an integrated, premium resume preview experience.
- Responsive Design & Adaptive Motion: Ensure the entire website is fully responsive, maintaining its premium feel and legibility across all devices (desktop, tablet, mobile), with motion behaviors gracefully adapting for optimal performance.
- Advanced Scroll & Motion Grammar: Integrate a consistent and aggressive but controlled motion language throughout the site, featuring scroll-triggered transforms, parallax, smooth transitions between sections, and reactive hover states.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A sophisticated, desaturated blue (#6B89CC) that conveys modern professionalism without being overly bold. HSL(220, 40%, 60%).
- Background Color: A deep, muted bluish-gray (#1A1D22) providing a premium dark mode canvas, offering subtle depth and allowing content to stand out. HSL(220, 15%, 12%).
- Accent Color: A vibrant, clear cyan (#56CBDE) used sparingly for interactive elements and key highlights, creating striking contrast. HSL(190, 70%, 55%).
- Headlines: 'Playfair' (serif), chosen for its elegant, high-contrast, and cinematic aesthetic, providing a luxurious feel.
- Body Text: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif), paired with Playfair for readability and a modern, warm touch, suitable for longer editorial content.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, minimalist vector icons that support the modern and refined aesthetic, avoiding overly complex or decorative styles.
- Employ a strong grid system with generous whitespace, deliberate pacing between sections, and controlled asymmetry to create a highly crafted, editorial layout that prioritizes content visibility and impact.
- Implement 'expensive' and purposeful motion through scroll-triggered scene changes, layered parallax, element staggering, and smooth state transitions, ensuring animations enhance rather than detract from usability.
